Cloud Knox

(Cloud Security Platform)

Overview

An application that reduces the risk in authorization and maintenance cost for large and medium scale Enterprises. This cloud security platform continuously monitors, assesses, adapts and responds to Identity and access management (IAM) risk as needed in real-time, It also provides a multi-dimensional view of risk by assessing identities, actions, and resources.

The Challenge

In dynamic cloud infrastructue, there is no comprehensive solution to control the risk and identity authorization

Our Solution

We created different iterations and offered a functional and beautiful solution which reduces the risk in authorization and 60% cost in maintainance
