About me and my Experience

I am Ravindra, User Experience Designer based in Hyderabad, India. I have a passion for creating user experiences that are seamless and impactful. I use human-centered design to create functional and beautiful user experiences.

Over the past five years, I worked in various domains such as ‘Healthcare, Networks, Cyber Security, Automotive, E-Commerce and Gaming’ from Idea to execution stage. During my journey, I teamed up with diverse people from diverse backgrounds, experiences, and disciplines which helped me to build my strong skills.

I enjoy using my skill set to empower people to accomplish their goals. I often found myself using creative solutions to save time, energy, and money.
